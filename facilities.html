Facilities

Gym

In effort to offer guests “the gym of the future,” the Oberoi has outfitted its fitness center with top-of-the-line cardio and strength-training equipment from Technogym’s elite fitness line, Oberoi. For this collection, Technogym’s scientific research department finessed the designs of each sleek machine to mimic the way bodies naturally move.

Swimming Pool

After a long day of exploring Udaipur, this swimming pool at the Cardamom County nature retreat is the perfect place to relax. Take in the stunning views of the surrounding scenery and breathe in the sumptuous scents from the spice plantations as you unwind and let your cares drift away.

Golf Course

Intricately landscaped greens and fairways have been set amidst the flowers, streams, and thousands of trees that shelter the course from the hustle and bustle of the golf course. With multiple tee options on every hole, the rolling greens and fairways provide a fair test of golf for players of all abilities.

Restaurant

Home to as many as six dining establishments, the hotel is renowned for its fine-dining restaurant called Le Cirque which serves French-Italian cuisine and has been host to innumerable celebrity guests, socialites and dignitaries from across the world.
