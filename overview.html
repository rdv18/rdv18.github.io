Overview

Who we Are ?



The Oberoi Hotels Resorts and Palaces is recognized the world over for delivering a unique flavor of hospitality that offers world-class refinement while remaining deeply rooted in its Indian heritage. For more than a century, The Oberoi has brought together the unique beauty and traditions from across India in an experience that highlights true Indian hospitality. This draws on the time-honored traditions central to the Indian homecoming, from the refreshing simplicity of the welcome drink to the careful choreography of the dinner service




What we Do?



With 100 hotels in 62 locations across India, The Oberoi offers guests the refined luxury of heritage combined with effortlessly efficient service and local expertise to create a moment to remember. In India, The Oberoi hotels are strategically located to offer the The Oberoi experience in business hubs, industrial towns and metropolises, as well as the most remote pilgrimage centers, historic and natural destinations. The group has extended its international presence with 15 hotels in the Maldives, Malaysia, the UK, USA, Bhutan, Sri Lanka, Africa and the Middle East.
